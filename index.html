Download the Straits Times Mobile App from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

  1. Sign in using the following credential:
  • Username: your email address
  • Password: Use the default password that has been emailed to you

  1. Change the default password after login.
  • Click on the "Forgot your password?" link to change password.
  • A link to reset your password will be sent to your email account.

Caution: It is important to change the default password if you do not want unauthorized access to your account.

Open a web browser, and go to the following url: https://www.straitstimes.com/. Click on the "LOG IN" link at the top of the webpage.

  1. Sign in using the following credential:
  • Username: your email address
  • Password: Use the default password that has been emailed to you
  1. Change the default password after login.
  • Click on the "Forgot your password?" link to change password.
  • A link to reset your password will be sent to your email account.
  1. Change the default password (Alternative Approach)
  • Click on your account name at the top of the webpage after you have logged in. You can update your password using the pop-up dialog.

Caution: It is important to change the default password if you do not want unauthorized access to your account.
